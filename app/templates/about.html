The American Mathematics Competitions (AMC) is a series of mathematics contests designed for middle and high school students to showcase their problem-solving abilities. Among the three levels, AMC12 is the most advanced, consisting of 25 problems that progressively increase in difficulty.

DailyAMC presents a new AMC12 problem each day, with Monday featuring the more accessible problems and Sunday's being the most difficult. It was made as graduation project in computer science.
